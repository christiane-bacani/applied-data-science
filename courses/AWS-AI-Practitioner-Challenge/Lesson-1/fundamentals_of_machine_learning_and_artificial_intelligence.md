### Fundamentals of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

#### 1. What is Generative AI?
    - Generative AI is a branch of **Artifical Intelligence** that focuses on creating new content such as text, image, audio, or even computer code from existing data. Unlike traditional AI systems or Machine Learning Models that analyze and interpret data, **Generative AI** learn new patterns and relationships from its huge amount of training data to create new content.
