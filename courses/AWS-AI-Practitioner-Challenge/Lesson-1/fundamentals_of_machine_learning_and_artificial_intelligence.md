### Fundamentals of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

#### 1. What is Generative AI?
    - Generative AI is a branch of **Artifical Intelligence** that focuses on creating new content such as text, image, audio, or even computer code from existing data. Unlike traditional AI systems or Machine Learning Models that analyze and interpret data, **Generative AI** learn new patterns and relationships from its huge amount of training data and use that knowledge to generate new content.

    Practical applications include:
        - **Text generation**: *Amazon Titan* or *Anthropic's Claude* can produce human-like writing on any kinds of topic.
        - **Computer Vision**: *Stable Diffusion* can create different images from simple text prompt.
        - **Audio and Speech Synthesis**: AI models can now generate human-like voices for virtual assistants, audiobooks, and even podcasts.
        - **Computer code generation**: Generative AI can assist developers by auto-completing *code snippets* or even generate entire programs based on prompt descriptions.
