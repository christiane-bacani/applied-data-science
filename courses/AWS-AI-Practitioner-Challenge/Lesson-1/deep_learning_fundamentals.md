# Deep Learning Fundamentals

The field of *deep learning* is inspired by the structure and functions of our brain, specifically it is called *neural networks* that mimics how our human brain processes information.


## Neural networks

The core of deep learning is neural network. Just like our brain have neurons that are connected to each other, the neural network have tiny units called *nodes*. The nodes are organized into layers. An input layer, one or more hidden layer, and an output layer.

![Neural Network Image](images/neural_network.png)

There are many examples of neural network, such as when a customer bought a product or services, the model starts to figure out patterns by adjusting the connections of each node. It's like the nodes are talking to each other in order to slowly figure out patterns about a certain product or services a customer will most likely buy.

When the neural network recognizes these patterns and starts making decisions or predictions on new customers it never seen before, it can still predict which most likely product or services they might want to buy.

The couple are branches of AI where deep learning is used to enhance results:

1. **Computer Vision**: A field of Artifical Intelligence that can interpret and analyze images and videos. Deep Learning empowers Computer Vision by providing techniques for task like image classification, object detection, and image segmentation.

2. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**: A field of Artifical Intelligence that deals with human language and machine. Deep learning continue to empower and make strides to Natural Language Processing by providing techniques for task like text classification, sentiment analysis, machine translation, and language generation.
